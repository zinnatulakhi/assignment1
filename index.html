Ntritional Services

Adequate nutrition is vital for the health and survival of all individuals.We provide a healthy food and a nutritional shake to orphans, vulnerable children

Livelihood Support

Form of livelihood strengthening, frequently funded by microloans or external partners that provide those living in poverty with an opportunity to develop a skill or product to be sold or offered as a form of livelihood.Examples include small-scale farming, bead making, and soap making.

Educational Project for a Orphan child

Educational project covers children's school fees, tutorial lessons, after school meals, school uniforms and materials. Children receive after school meals and tutorial lessons at our drop in center with a library and other services.This project helps these children build a bright future through quality education and holistic care.
